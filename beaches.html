Carolina Beach

Carolina Beach is at the north end of Pleasure Island. It is also the home of Freeman Park and Carolina Beach State Park. In addition, Carolina Beach offers a great location to swim, fish, kayak, boat, surf, or bike. But, if you're in the mood for a nice party Carolina Beach offers a party boat tour, restaurants with live music, and arcades full of fun.

Events & Activities:

  • Beach Music Festival
  • Fireworks by the Sea
  • Free Movies at the Lake
  • Seafood, Blues, and Jazz Festival

Kure Beach

Kure Beach is located to the south of Carolina Beach. Kure Beach is mostly a residential town with beautiful cottages and condos. However, it is also a town with a beautiful beach to sunbathe, surf, and fish.

Events & Activities:

  • Annual Fishing Tournament
  • Bountiful Surf Fishing

Historic Fort Fisher

Fort Fisher is located to the south of Pleasure Island. Fort Fisher is North Carolina's third most visited historical location. Travel back in time by visiting one of the museums, or enjoy the present with the many ferry rides, aquarium location, or recreation areas.

Events & Activities:

  • Fort Fisher Ferry
  • Fort Fisher Recreation Area
  • Fort Fisher Historic Site
  • Fort Fisher Aquarium
